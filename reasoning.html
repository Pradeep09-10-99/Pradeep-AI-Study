Logical Reasoning

NTPC, Group D aur Railway Exams ke liye Top 5 Logical Questions.

Reasoning Syllabus

  • 🧩 Coding - Decoding
  • 👥 Blood Relations (रक्त संबंध)
  • 🔄 Syllogism (कथन व निष्कर्ष)
  • 🔢 Number & Alphabet Series
  • 🧭 Direction Sense Test

Q1. Coding - Decoding

Agar ek khaas code me 'CHAIR' ko 'EJCKT' likha jata hai, toh 'LIGHT' ko kya likha jayega?

Q2. Blood Relation (रक्त संबंध)

Ek ladke ki tasveer ki or ishara karte hue Suresh ne kaha, "Yeh mere dada ji ke iklote bete ka beta hai." Suresh ka us ladke se kya sambandh hai?

Q3. Number Series (संख्या श्रृंखला)

Is series ka agla pad kya hoga?
2, 6, 12, 20, 30, ?

Q4. Direction Sense (दिशा परीक्षण)

Rohan uttar (North) ki or 10 km chalta hai, fir wo apne daayein (Right) mudta hai aur 5 km chalta hai. Ab wo kis disha me ja raha hai?

Q5. Wrong Number Find (गलत संख्या पहचानें)

Niche di gayi series me kaun si sankhya galat (Wrong) hai?
4, 9, 16, 25, 35, 49
