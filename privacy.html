Privacy Policy

Pradeep AI Study par aapki privacy hamare liye bahut zaroori hai. Is document mein bataya gaya hai ki hum kis tarah ki information collect karte hain aur uska istemal kaise kiya jata hai.

1. Information We Collect

Jab aap hamare Student Portal par register karte hain, toh hum aapka email address aur account activity data securely store karte hain taaki aap apna study dashboard track kar sakein.

2. Log Files aur Cookies

Baki sabhi websites ki tarah, hum bhi standard log files aur cookies ka use karte hain. Isme IP address, browser type, aur page visit timing track hoti hai taaki hum user experience ko aur behtar bana sakein.

3. Google AdSense & Third-Party Ads

Hamari website par Google AdSense ke ads dikhaye ja sakte hain. Google dynamic ads dikhane ke liye DART cookies ka use karta hai. Aap chahein toh Google ad settings mein jaakar personalized ads ko disable kar sakte hain.

4. Contact Us

Agar is policy se juda koi bhi sawal hai, toh aap hamare Contact Form ke zariye humse connect kar sakte hain.
