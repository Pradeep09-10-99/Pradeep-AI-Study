RRB NTPC Exam First Attempt Mein Crack Kaise Karein: Full Strategy Guide

May 24, 2026 By Pradeep Kumar 5 Min Read

Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) NTPC ek aisi exam hai jisme har saal lakhon students baithte hain, is wajah se competition bahut zyada badh jata hai. Lekin agar aapke paas ek sahi direction aur perfect plan ho, toh aap apne First Attempt mein hi is exam ko clear kar sakte hain. Is article mein hum baat karenge un specific tips aur subject-wise strategy ki jo aapko selection dilwayengi.

1. Exam Pattern Ko Samajhna Sabse Zaroori Hai

Kisi bhi ladaai ko jeetne ke liye dushman ki takat ka pata hona chahiye, thik vaise hi NTPC clear karne ke liye CBT-1 aur CBT-2 ka pattern clear hona chahiye. Niche diye gaye table se samjhein kis subject ka kitna weightage hai:

Subject CBT-1 (Marks) CBT-2 (Marks)
General Awareness (GK/GS) 40 Marks 50 Marks
Mathematics 30 Marks 35 Marks
General Intelligence & Reasoning 30 Marks 35 Marks
Total 100 Marks 120 Marks

⚠️ Negative Marking Ka Dhyan Rakhein!

NTPC exam mein 1/3 Negative Marking hoti hai. Iska matlab hai ki har 3 galat answers par aapka 1 sahi mark kaat liya jayega. Isliye tukke lagane se bachein aur accuracy par focus karein.

2. Subject-Wise Strategy (Kaise Padhein?)

  • Mathematics: Sabse pehle Calculation speed badhayein. Aapko kam se kam 30 tak Tables aur 50 tak Squares yaad hone chahiye. Profit & Loss, Percentage, Simple Interest aur Time & Work jaise chapters par apni solid pakad banayein.
  • Reasoning: Ye section sabse zyada scoring hota hai. Coding-Decoding, Syllogism, Blood Relation, aur Blood Relation ke questions par daily 1 ghanta practice karein. Hamare portal par diye gaye mock tests aapki logical thinking badhane mein madad karenge.
  • General Science & GK: CBT-1 aur CBT-2 dono mein iska weightage sabse bada hai. Physics, Chemistry aur Biology ke basic concepts ko NCERT se clear karein aur pichle 1 saal ke Current Affairs regular padhein.

3. Mock Tests Aur Revision Ki Ahmiyat

Sirf padhte rehne se selection nahi hota. Aapko har hafte kam se kam 2 full-length Mock Tests dene chahiye. Test dene ke baad uska ache se analysis karein ki aapki galti kahan ho rahi hai. Jo question galat ho rahe hain, unhe dobara notebook mein solve karein.

Aap hamari website ke Mega Mock Test section par jaakar bilkul free mein dynamic quiz de sakte hain, jahan real exam jaisa timer chalta hai.

Conclusion

Consistency hi kamyabi ki chabi hai. Agar aap daily 6 se 8 ghante lagatar bina break liye mehnat karte hain, toh NTPC 2026 mein aapki ek seat pakki hai. Apni padhai ko boost karne ke liye hamare AI Chatbot se kabhi bhi apne doubts pooch sakte hain.

All the best! Taiyari rukni nahi chahiye.
